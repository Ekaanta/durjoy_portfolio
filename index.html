01

Muslim Prayer & Qibla Direction App

📍 Client: USA-based — Personal Islamic App

A US-based client wanted a personal Muslim prayer app after struggling to find accurate Qibla direction from his location. I took full project ownership — from initial client briefing to final delivery — managing the entire development lifecycle across a cross-functional team.

🔍
The Problem
The app's Qibla compass was pointing in the wrong direction. The developer couldn't identify why. After multiple failed fixes, the client was frustrated. I stepped in, conducted a deep-dive meeting with the client, and traced the root cause: the app was using generic coordinates instead of the client's actual GPS latitude and longitude.
🎯
My Role
Ran structured client meetings, documented 13+ issues in a tracked Google Sheet, prioritized them by severity, communicated each fix to the development team, and verified resolution. Built a full issue log with status tracking (Fixed / Still Issues) and video evidence links.
✅
The Outcome
All reported issues resolved. Qibla direction now accurately reflects the user's real-time GPS location. Client left a 5-star review on Fiverr, praising communication, deep understanding, and delivery quality.
